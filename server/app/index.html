Welcome to Cryptic

Private, secure communication made simple. Connect with friends or collaborators without unnecessary complexity or centralized control

Why Cryptic?

  • Private and secure: Peer-to-peer connections keep your conversations between you and the people you’re talking to.
  • Open source: Explore the code, contribute, or build your own projects using Cryptic’s foundation.
  • Fast setup: Get started quickly, no complicated configuration required.
  • Flexible: Use it for messaging, voice calls, video calls, or experimenting with WebRTC in your own apps.

Get Started

Whether you’re experimenting with peer-to-peer communication or just want a private chat, Cryptic makes it simple:

  • Try the platform directly using the public domain "signalingserverdomain.download"
  • Explore the source code on GitHub and contribute to the project.
  • Join the community on Discord for support and discussion.

Who We Are

Cryptic is built by a small team of developers passionate about privacy and open-source communication. We’re constantly improving the platform and welcome contributions from anyone interested in helping it grow.
